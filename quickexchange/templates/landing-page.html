What is QuickExchange?

QuickExchange offers a fast, reliable, and secure way of transfering files or URL links from one device to another.

QuickExchange is entirely web-based, no need to download any sort of application on your laptop or mobile device.

Need an app that can transfer files from one device to another? Whether it is a photo, video, text file, or a powerpoint presentation, QuickExchange allows you to transfer your files across devices!

How it works

When you login with your new account, you are given a what we call a Stack.

Simply login to QuickExchange from your phone and push your file onto your personal Stack.

Then from your laptop, or any other device, go onto the same QuickExchange app and pop your file out so you can view/download it.
